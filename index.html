About Us

Auk Impex Formulation is a team of Wholesale Business of Industrial Aerosol Products

We deals in Industrial Aerosol Products and Industrial Lubricants & Greases.

  • Deals in Industrial Lubricants
  • Deals in Industrial Spraying Lubricants and Industrial MRO Lubricants

Foundation of Auk Impex Formulation was laid in 2015 by Mr. Himank Sharma. Auk Impex wants to be the preferred global speciality lubricants provider. Our focus is to drive productivity and efficiency in our customers through technology, innovation and sustainability.

In pursuing business excellence, Auk Impex Formulation is committed to supply genuine lubricants and ensure timely delivery through our distribution network.
We are a professionally managed organization, engaged in the supplying technically advanced products for a range of renowned brands like Gulf Oil Lubricants, MolyGraphs, OKS, Hindustan Petroleum, Castrol etc.
With more than 10 years of experience in the respective sector, our distribution network has been delivering the best in Ghaziabad and Noida.

Services

Since 2011, Auk Impex Formulation, an Authorized wholesale dealer in Ghaziabad has been dealing in all types of Lubricants and Industrial Aerosol Products.

Industrial Lubricants

Auk offers a range of gear oils and lubricants in different chemistries & viscosities, specially formulated for lubrication of enclosed gear boxes.

MRO Products

The maintenance, repair and operations of machinery and equipment used in an integrated supply chain management model.

Greases

We provide high qualities greases with Solid lubricants, EP and AW additives. They reduce friction, wear and tear and offer good rust protection, that result in the reduction of overall maintenance costs.
